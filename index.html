About the Show

Introduction

Deep Debugging is an adult animated comedy series that dives into the chaotic and humorous world of developers working on a simulation of our universe.

Follow the antics of a quirky team as they navigate through digital glitches, unexpected bugs, and the everyday absurdities of debugging.

Plot

In a world where every line of code can lead to chaos, Deep Debugging takes you on a rollercoaster ride through a simulated universe that’s more unpredictable than reality itself.

Our heroes venture into the simulation to fix bugs, only to find themselves caught in hilarious situations that challenge their coding skills and sanity.

J

A young, awkward, and inexperienced developer. J approaches his work with great enthusiasm and is eager to prove himself. However, due to his limited life experience, he struggles with social norms and often misinterprets what is expected of him.

Tom

A senior developer and Jay's mentor, Tom is a super-professional with the charisma of a weary rock star. He is brutally honest and somewhat self-assured, but he genuinely cares about his team members and takes his mentorship role very seriously.

Polly

A developer with an intriguing geeky appearance, Polly is known for her seriousness and strong sense of responsibility.

Archie

A developer with a cheerful and full-figured presence, Archie is a constant jokester who always has a smile on his face.

Boss

The founder of the UniDev company, the Boss is a visionary who wrote the first version of the simulation in his garage in just one week. He is known for making bold and expressive management decisions and has an unshakeable belief in his limitless genius. However, he rarely acknowledges his mistakes.

Elon Musk
